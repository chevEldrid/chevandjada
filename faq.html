FAQ

Common questions about our wedding day

If you have any other questions than what we've answered below, please reach out to Chev or Jada.

What should I wear?

Formal attire preferred. For women, ankle-length or elevated midi (below knee) dresses. For men, tuxedos or suits with ties. We recognize our wedding is in the summer and what is most important is that our guests are comfortable and ready to dance.

Can I bring a Plus One?

We are so excited to celebrate with our closest family and friends. Due to limited space, we are only able to accommodate those listed on your invitation. If a plus one has been included, it will be indicated on your invitation. We appreciate your understanding and can’t wait to see you!

Can I take photos/video during the ceremony?

We kindly request that all guests put away their phones and cameras during the ceremony. We want you to be fully present with us during this time. Don’t worry, our photographer will capture every special moment and we can’t wait to share those memories with you.

What accommodations are available for out-of-town guests?
